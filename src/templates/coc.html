Code of Conduct (COC)

PythonBD is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members of our community. We expect all participants to treat each other with respect, professionalism, and kindness.

Our community values diversity, encourages learning, and promotes collaboration among Python enthusiasts of all skill levels.

By participating in PythonBD events and discussions, you agree to abide by our community guidelines and help us maintain a positive space for everyone.
